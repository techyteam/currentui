S.F.P CREDENTIAL

SUSTAINABILITY FACILITY PROFESSIONAL

IFMA’s Sustainability Facility Professional (SFP) is an assessment-based certificate program delivering a specialty credential in sustainability. It’s also an opportunity for FMs with an interest in efficiency, data-driven decision-making and sustainable practices. By earning your SFP designation, you will develop and gain recognition for your expertise in sustainable FM practices while impacting your organization’s economic, environmental and social bottom lines.

HOW DO I EARN THE SFP?

The Sustainability Facility Professional® Full Kit, available with printed book or digital only, is the only tool you’ll need to earn your SFP credential in one complete program. This interactive program will teach the knowledge, skills and tools needed to develop sustainable facility management practices and also includes the final SFP assessments required to earn the SFP credential. Now IFMA is offering more choices when you purchase the SFP Credential Program®. You can purchase the Full SFP Learning System (includes all 3 modules) or buy one module at a time. Your Options Are:



  • SFP Learning System- Full Kit

  • SFP - Managing Sustainable Facilities Only

  • SFP - Operating Sustainable Facilities Only

  • SFP - Strategy and Alignment Only
